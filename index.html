Providing a digital comminication channel for rental tenants.

repo: github.com/jamespankhurst/letcomm

In the pre-internet ninties, I was involved in developing insurance claim software, with particular reference to property maintenance and tenant communication.

I developed the first web version of this software in classic ASP, claimtrack.co.uk., then re-coded for .NET to produce; trackpro.co.uk,the current version.

Even though the system was now internet based, the benefits remained with the landlords/property management companies (PMCs).

The system issues 'access keys' to third parties; roofers, builders, surveyors....but NEVER tenants.
