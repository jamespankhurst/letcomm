In the late ninties, I was involved in developing terristrial insurance claim software, with particular reference to property maintenance.

In the early 2000's I developed the first web version of this software in vbScript, classic ASP, (no Longer active claimtrack.co.uk).

I then re-coded for vb.NET, ASP.NET to produce http://www.trackpro.co.uk,the current version.

It gave the landlord/property management company control of daily issues; roof leaks, gutters blocked, broken windows, etc.

The system issued 'access keys' to third parties; roofers, builders, surveyors....but NEVER tenants.
